Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking.

What's the difference between lifestyle and regular product videography?

Lifestyle videos show your product or brand in real-world, aspirational contexts — focusing on feelings and experiences. Product videos focus on the item itself. Lifestyle content converts better for social media.

Do you handle content creation for influencers?

Yes. We work with nano, micro, and macro influencers to create high-quality content for brand collaborations and their own channels.

What locations do you shoot in Delhi NCR?

We shoot at Lodhi Garden, Hauz Khas Village, Connaught Place, rooftop venues, cafés, studios, and any custom location of your choice.

Can you shoot content for a full month's social media calendar?

Absolutely. Our monthly content packages cover planning, shooting, and editing enough content to fill your social media for an entire month.
