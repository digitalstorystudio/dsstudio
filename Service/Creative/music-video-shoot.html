Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking.

Do you handle casting for the music video?

Yes. We have a network of models, dancers, and actors available for music video casting. Alternatively, you can cast your own people and we direct them on set.

Can you shoot music videos in outdoor locations?

Absolutely. We scout and shoot at outdoor locations across Delhi NCR including Lodhi Garden, Old Delhi, Rajasthan, and hill locations in Himachal Pradesh for destination shoots.

Do you produce Punjabi and Hindi music videos?

Yes. We have extensive experience producing Punjabi pop, Hindi film-style, and Sufi music videos with appropriate aesthetics and locations for each genre.

What camera and equipment do you use for music videos?

We shoot on cinema-grade cameras (Sony FX6/FX3, Canon EOS C70) with prime and zoom lenses, gimbal stabilizers, and professional LED lighting for cinematic results.
