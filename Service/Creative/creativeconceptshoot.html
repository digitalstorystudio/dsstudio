Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking.

How developed does my concept need to be before booking?

You can come with anything — a fully detailed brief, a rough idea, or just a feeling you want to capture. Our creative directors will help develop and refine the concept before the shoot.

Can you create composite images?

Yes. Our post-production team is skilled in Photoshop compositing, background replacement, sky replacement, and creating impossible visual scenarios from multiple exposures.

How long does a complex creative shoot take to plan?

Simple creative shoots can be planned in 1 week. Complex shoots with custom sets, multiple models, or elaborate props typically need 2–4 weeks of pre-production. Contact us early.

Do you create content for art exhibitions?

Yes. We produce gallery-standard fine art prints, exhibition installation photography, and video art for artists and galleries. Contact us with your exhibition brief.
