Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking.

What is the best time to photograph a property?

Mid-morning light (10am–12pm) works best for most properties. For exteriors, golden hour (1hr after sunrise or before sunset) gives dramatic results. Twilight shots are also available.

Do you stage or style the property before shooting?

We provide staging guidance as part of our service. For premium shoots, we can arrange professional home staging at an additional cost.

Can you photograph multiple properties on the same day?

Yes. We offer multi-property day packages for real estate agents and developers. Contact us to plan an efficient shooting schedule.

Do you produce virtual tours compatible with major portals?

Yes. Our virtual tours are compatible with Google Street View, Matterport-style viewers, and embedded players for 99acres, MagicBricks, and Housing.com.
