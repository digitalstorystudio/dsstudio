Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my pre-wedding shoot?
We recommend booking at least 2–3 months before your desired shoot date, especially during the wedding season (Oct–Feb). Early booking ensures you get your preferred date and location.
What locations do you suggest for pre-wedding shoots in Delhi NCR?
We love Lodhi Garden, Qutub Minar, Humayun's Tomb, Hauz Khas Village, Sunder Nursery, Lodi Art District, India Gate, and rooftop spots in Connaught Place. We also do outskirts like Sultanpur, Damdama Lake, and Neemrana Fort.
How many photos will I receive?
Depending on your package, you'll receive 100 to 300+ professionally edited, high-resolution photos delivered via a private online gallery.
Do you travel for destination pre-wedding shoots?
Yes! We regularly travel to Agra, Udaipur, Jaipur, Mussoorie, Rishikesh, and more. Travel packages are customised based on location.
Can we use props or have a themed shoot?
Absolutely! We encourage personalised themes — Bollywood, vintage, fairy-tale, royal Mughal, and more. We help with concept direction and can connect you with prop vendors.
