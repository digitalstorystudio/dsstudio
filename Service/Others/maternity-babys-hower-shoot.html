Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking.

When is the best time to do a maternity shoot?

We recommend between 28–34 weeks of pregnancy. The bump is beautifully visible, and most mothers feel comfortable enough to move and pose. Earlier or later sessions are also possible based on your preference.

What should I wear for a maternity photoshoot?

Form-fitting dresses, flowy gowns, or fabric wraps work beautifully. We provide fabric wraps and props as part of the shoot. Wear something you feel confident and comfortable in.

Do you cover baby shower events?

Yes. We provide full event photography and candid coverage of baby shower events — from décor details and guests to games, gifts, and cake cutting moments.

Can my partner and other children be included in the shoot?

Absolutely. Couple and family maternity sessions including existing children are among our most cherished shoots. We encourage including your whole family in at least part of the session.
