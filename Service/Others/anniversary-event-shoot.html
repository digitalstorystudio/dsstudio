Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking.

Can you create a film combining our wedding and anniversary footage?

Yes. Our editors can blend your anniversary shoot footage with your original wedding photos/video to create a beautiful 'then and now' anniversary film.

Do you coordinate surprise anniversary shoots?

Absolutely. We have experience coordinating surprise shoots — working with family members to set up the location and guide the couple without revealing the plan.

Can you shoot at our preferred restaurant or venue?

Yes. We shoot at any location you choose — restaurants, gardens, heritage sites, or your home. We do an advance location scout for all venue-based shoots.

Do you offer outstation anniversary shoots?

Yes. We travel for destination anniversary shoots across India. Travel costs are calculated separately. Popular destinations include Agra, Jaipur, Rishikesh, and Shimla.
