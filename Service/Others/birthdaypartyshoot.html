Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking.

Do you do cake smash photography for first birthdays?

Yes. Cake smash sessions are one of our most popular services for first birthdays. We set up a themed backdrop and props and guide the session to capture those adorable messy moments.

Can you photograph a surprise birthday party?

Yes. For surprise parties, we arrive early, blend in with the setup, and are positioned to capture the exact moment of surprise. We coordinate with the organiser to ensure perfect timing.

Do you cover outdoor birthday events?

Absolutely. We cover birthday events at gardens, farmhouses, rooftop venues, restaurants, and any outdoor location. We bring portable lighting for evening events.

Can you create a birthday highlight reel for social media?

Yes. Our birthday reels are edited specifically for Instagram and WhatsApp sharing — vertical format, upbeat music, and a length of 60–90 seconds for maximum engagement.
