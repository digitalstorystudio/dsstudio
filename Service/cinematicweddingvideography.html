Frequently Asked Questions

What is cinematic wedding videography?

Cinematic wedding videography is a style that creates film-like wedding videos using professional cameras, cinematic techniques, storytelling elements, and high-quality post-production. Unlike traditional wedding videos, cinematic films focus on emotions, artistic shots, and creating a movie-like experience of your wedding day.

How much does cinematic wedding videography cost in Delhi?

Cinematic wedding videography in Delhi typically ranges from ₹75,000 to ₹2,50,000 depending on the package, duration, number of events, and specific requirements. We offer customized packages to fit different budgets while maintaining our high-quality standards.

Do you provide drone videography for weddings?

Yes, we provide professional drone videography for weddings in Delhi NCR. Our drone coverage includes aerial shots of the venue, couple portraits, grand entrances, and cinematic establishing shots. All drone operations comply with local regulations and venue permissions.

What's included in your cinematic wedding packages?

Our cinematic wedding packages typically include 4K filming, multiple camera angles, professional editing, color grading, background music, raw footage delivery, and highlight films. Premium packages also include drone coverage, same-day edits, and pre-wedding shoots.

How long does it take to receive the final wedding film?

We deliver cinematic wedding films within 4-6 weeks of your wedding date. For urgent requirements, we offer express delivery options. Same-day highlight reels are available for reception viewing on the wedding day itself.

Do you cover destination weddings outside Delhi?

Yes, we provide destination wedding videography across India and internationally. Our team has covered weddings in Rajasthan, Goa, Himachal Pradesh, and other exotic locations. Travel and accommodation costs are additional to the package price.
