Frequently Asked Questions

What is cinematic wedding videography?

Cinematic wedding videography is a filmmaking approach that treats your wedding as a feature film — with deliberate camera movements, artistic framing, colour grading, musical scoring, and a narrative story arc that brings your memories to life like a movie.

How long is the delivered wedding film?

Highlight films are typically 5–10 minutes. Full documentary wedding films are 45–90 minutes depending on coverage. Same-day edit teasers are 2–3 minutes delivered on the wedding evening.

Do you include drone footage?

Yes! All packages include drone aerial footage of the venue and ceremonies (subject to local airspace regulations). Drone shots add a grand, cinematic perspective that makes your film truly spectacular.

How soon will we receive our wedding film?

Highlight films are delivered within 4 weeks. Full-length documentary films within 8 weeks. Same-day edit teasers are delivered before the wedding concludes.

Do you cover multi-day weddings?

Absolutely. We cover Mehendi, Haldi, Sangeet, Wedding, and Reception across multiple days. Custom multi-day packages are available for all ceremony combinations.
