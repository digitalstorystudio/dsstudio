Traditional Wedding Videography FAQ

Do you understand traditional Hindu wedding rituals?

Yes, our team has extensive experience with Hindu wedding ceremonies including all rituals from Haldi to Vidaai. We understand the significance of each ceremony and work closely with families and pundits to ensure respectful coverage.

Can you film inside Gurudwaras for Sikh weddings?

We have experience filming Sikh Anand Karaj ceremonies in various Gurudwaras across Delhi. We always seek proper permissions and follow Gurudwara protocols while maintaining the sanctity of the ceremony.

How do you capture traditional Punjabi celebrations?

Our approach to Punjabi wedding videography focuses on the vibrant energy, cultural performances, and family emotions. We use multiple cameras to capture Bhangra performances and cultural celebrations from various angles.

Do you provide traditional music with the films?

Yes, we record authentic wedding mantras, kirtan, and cultural songs during ceremonies and include them in the final film to preserve the spiritual and cultural atmosphere of your traditional wedding.

What's included in traditional wedding packages?

Our traditional wedding packages include complete ceremony coverage, pre-wedding rituals, professional editing, highlight films, raw footage, and cultural storytelling approach tailored to your specific traditions.

How far in advance should we book?

We recommend booking 3-6 months in advance for traditional weddings, especially during peak season (October to March). This ensures availability and allows time for detailed planning of your cultural ceremonies.
