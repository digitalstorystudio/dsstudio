Frequently Asked Questions

What is candid photography?
Candid photography is a documentary style where the photographer captures real, unscripted moments without posing subjects. It results in authentic, emotional images that tell the true story of your wedding day.
How is candid photography different from traditional wedding photography?
Traditional wedding photography involves posed portraits and staged group photos. Candid photography focuses on natural, spontaneous moments — tears, laughter, secret glances — captured without the subject being aware of the camera.
How many photographers will be present?
Our Signature and Premium packages include two experienced photographers working simultaneously to ensure full coverage from multiple angles — especially during high-energy moments like Baraat and Jaimala.
How long before we receive our photos?
You'll receive a preview gallery of 10–20 selected images within 48 hours. The full edited gallery is delivered within 3–4 weeks of your wedding.
Do you cover pre-wedding events too?
Yes! We can cover Mehendi, Haldi, Sangeet, and all pre-wedding functions in addition to the main wedding day. Custom multi-day packages are available.
