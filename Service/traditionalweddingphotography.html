Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have experience with different religious wedding traditions?
Yes. We have extensively covered Hindu weddings (Punjabi, Rajasthani, South Indian, Bengali), Sikh Anand Karaj ceremonies, Muslim Nikah weddings, and Christian church weddings — each with deep knowledge of customs.
How do you handle large family group photos?
We pre-plan group combinations, use clear communication, and employ professional posing techniques to ensure everyone looks their best and no one is missed from family portraits.
Can we combine traditional and candid photography?
Absolutely! Our Gold and Platinum packages include both formal traditional photography and natural candid coverage — giving you the very best of both photography styles.
Do you provide printed photos and albums?
Yes. All packages include printed photo sets or traditional wedding albums. Premium hardbound albums with custom design are available in Gold and Platinum packages.
How many photographers will be present at the wedding?
Silver includes 1 photographer. Gold and Platinum packages include 2 photographers working simultaneously to ensure zero moments are missed during the ceremony.
