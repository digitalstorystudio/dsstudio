Types of Wedding Films We Create

Wedding Teaser (90 sec)

A punchy, high-energy 60-90 second teaser trailer that captures the essence of your wedding — perfect for sharing on Instagram and WhatsApp immediately.

Highlight Reel (3-5 min)

A beautifully edited 3-5 minute highlight film showcasing the best moments — from getting ready to the first dance, perfectly synced to music.

Same-Day Edit

A special highlight reel created and played at your reception on the same wedding day — surprising your guests with a mini film of the day!

Social Media Reels

Vertical format 30-60 second reels for Instagram, Facebook, and YouTube Shorts to share your wedding story with the world instantly.

Feature Highlight (7-10 min)

A comprehensive highlight covering all key ceremonies and moments — more detailed than a standard reel but shorter than a full documentary.

Reception Screening Film

A specially crafted film for screening at your reception — often combining pre-wedding footage with wedding day moments for maximum impact.

Our Editing Style

Perfect Music Sync

Every beat drop, every lyric, every emotional swell perfectly synced to the moments that match — creating a film that gives goosebumps.

Cinematic Color Grade

Hollywood-grade color grading that sets the mood — warm for romantic moments, vibrant for celebrations, dramatic for emotional ones.

Tight Editing

Every second of the highlight is deliberate. No fluff, no filler — just the most powerful, emotional, and beautiful moments.

Couple-Focused

Your story is the hero. We ensure you and your partner are the center of every highlight film we create.
