Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our destination wedding videography services

Do you travel outside India for destination weddings?

Yes! We have extensive experience covering destination weddings across India and internationally. We've filmed weddings in Dubai, Bali, Thailand, Singapore, Malaysia, and several European destinations. Our team handles all travel logistics and carries professional equipment worldwide.

What is the cost of destination wedding videography from Delhi?

Our destination wedding packages start from ₹2,50,000 and vary based on location, number of days, team size, and deliverables. Packages include travel, accommodation for our team, complete coverage, 4K/8K filming, drone footage, and edited films. Contact us for a customized quote based on your specific requirements.

How early should we book for Goa/Jaipur/Udaipur weddings?

We recommend booking at least 4-6 months in advance for popular destinations like Goa, Jaipur, and Udaipur, especially during peak wedding season (October-March). This allows us to plan location research, arrange permits, and ensure availability of our best team members for your dates.

Do we need to arrange travel and accommodation for your team?

Yes, couples typically arrange accommodation and meals for our team at the wedding venue or nearby hotels. Travel costs are included in our package pricing. We coordinate with your wedding planner to ensure smooth logistics. Our team is professional, self-sufficient, and requires minimal support.

What equipment do you bring for destination weddings?

We carry complete professional gear including multiple 4K/8K cinema cameras, drones (with necessary permits), gimbals, slider rigs, professional lighting equipment, wireless audio systems, and backup batteries. All equipment is insured and travel-safe. We also bring backup gear to ensure nothing is missed.

How long does it take to receive the final wedding film?

Teaser films are delivered within 2-3 weeks, and the complete cinematic wedding film is delivered within 60-90 days. For same-day edits, highlights are screened during your reception. We also provide raw footage and additional edits as per your package. Rush delivery options are available at additional cost.

Do you provide drone videography for all destinations?

We provide drone coverage wherever legally permitted. Our licensed drone pilots obtain necessary permissions for domestic and international locations. Some venues and cities have restrictions, which we research in advance. Drone footage adds breathtaking aerial perspectives to your wedding film.

Can you coordinate with our destination wedding planner?

Absolutely! We work closely with wedding planners, decorators, and venue coordinators to ensure seamless coverage. Pre-wedding coordination calls help us understand timelines, locations, and special moments to capture. Our collaborative approach ensures stress-free filming without disrupting your celebrations.
