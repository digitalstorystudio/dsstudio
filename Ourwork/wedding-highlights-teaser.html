Frequently Asked Questions — Wedding Highlights & Teasers

What is the difference between a wedding highlights video and a full wedding film?

A wedding highlights video (3–8 minutes) is a carefully edited, music-driven, narrative-focused short film that captures the best moments of your wedding day — the laughter, tears, first dance, vows, and celebrations. It is designed to be watched repeatedly and shared widely. A full wedding film (90–180 minutes) is a comprehensive documentary-style recording of every event, ritual, and speech in chronological order. Most couples use the highlights film for social sharing and the full film for personal archival. Digital Story Studio excels in both formats, serving couples across Delhi, Noida, Gurgaon, and all NCR locations.

How quickly can I get my wedding teaser video after the wedding in Delhi NCR?

Our standard wedding teaser delivery is within 72 hours for Silver and Gold packages. For Platinum packages, we offer a same-day teaser edit (SDE) — screened at your reception — plus a polished social media version within 24 hours. We understand that couples in Delhi, Noida, Gurgaon, and NCR want to share their moments immediately on Instagram and WhatsApp, so we prioritize fast turnaround without compromising quality.

What is the ideal length for a wedding highlight reel in India?

The ideal length for a wedding highlight reel in India depends on the number of events. For a single-day wedding (baraat + ceremony + reception), a 3–5 minute highlight film works perfectly. For multi-day celebrations including Mehendi, Sangeet, Haldi, Wedding, and Reception — a 6–10 minute cinematic film is recommended. For social media teasers, we always create separate 60–90 second cuts optimized for Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts. Our team serves all formats across Delhi NCR locations.

Do you cover multi-day weddings and pre-wedding shoots for highlight reels?

Absolutely! We specialize in multi-day wedding coverage across Delhi NCR — covering Mehendi, Haldi, Sangeet, Cocktail, Wedding, and Reception with separate highlight reels or a combined multi-event film. We also incorporate pre-wedding shoot footage into your highlight reel as an opening cinematic sequence, which creates an incredible narrative arc from courtship to celebration. We are available at all banquet halls, farmhouses, hotels, and outdoor venues across Delhi, Noida, Gurgaon, Faridabad, and Ghaziabad.

Can I choose the music for my wedding highlight video?

Yes! Music selection is one of the most personal aspects of your highlight film. You can suggest Bollywood songs, regional music, international tracks, or instrumental pieces. Our music team will license the chosen track commercially to ensure your video can be publicly shared without copyright strikes on YouTube and Instagram. If you're unsure about music choices, our editors will recommend tracks from our premium licensed music library that perfectly match your wedding's mood, theme, and pace.

What formats will my wedding highlight video be delivered in?

Digital Story Studio delivers your wedding highlight film in multiple formats: 4K Ultra HD master file for full-quality archiving, 1080p Full HD for streaming and playback, Instagram-optimized 1080×1920 vertical format for Reels, WhatsApp compressed version for easy sharing, and a YouTube-ready 16:9 format with optional private/public link. All files are delivered via secure private cloud link for easy download from anywhere in India or abroad.

Do you provide drone coverage for wedding teasers and highlights in Delhi NCR?

Yes! We include professional drone cinematography in our Gold and Platinum highlight packages. Our licensed drone pilots operate DJI Mavic 3 Cine and DJI Mini 4 Pro units to capture sweeping aerial views of your venue — whether it's a farmhouse in Bijwasan, a banquet in Noida, a resort in Gurgaon, or a hotel in Faridabad. Drone permits are arranged in advance for venues where approval is required. Aerial footage is seamlessly integrated into the highlight reel for cinematic grandeur.

How much does a wedding highlight reel cost in Delhi?

Wedding highlight reel pricing in Delhi depends on the package, number of events, and coverage requirements. Our Silver Teaser package is ideal for budget-conscious couples wanting a quality social media teaser. Our Gold Highlights package (most popular) covers full-day weddings with a 3–5 min film plus teaser. Our Platinum Cinematic package covers multi-day grand weddings with full production.
