Expert Model Photoshoot Tips & Guide for Delhi Models

Insider advice from Delhi NCR's top model photographers to help you prepare for your best shoot ever

01

Prepare Multiple Outfits in Advance

Bring at least 3–5 outfits including formal, casual, ethnic, and athleisure to showcase range. Iron everything the night before. Neutral and jewel-toned colours photograph best on camera.

02

Skin Prep Matters More Than Makeup

Hydrate well for 3 days before your shoot. Use a gentle face mask the night before. Avoid new skincare products. Clean, moisturised skin picks up light beautifully and reduces retouching time.

03

Practice Poses in Front of a Mirror

Know your best angles. Practice S-curves, chin-forward poses, hand placements, and dynamic walking shots. The more familiar you are with your body's geometry, the more natural you look on camera.

04

Communicate With Your Photographer

Share your vision, references, and goals before the shoot. A good shoot is collaborative. Tell us what kind of modelling work you're targeting — we'll tailor the direction accordingly.

05

Get Enough Sleep the Night Before

Fatigue shows in the eyes and skin tone. 8 hours of sleep the night before ensures you look bright, energetic, and naturally radiant — no amount of retouching replaces genuine rest.

06

Know Your Comp Card Requirements

For agency submissions, a standard comp card needs: full-length, half-length, close-up face, profile, and one action/lifestyle shot. Our Professional Portfolio package covers all 5 required frames.

07

Choose the Right Shoot Time

For outdoor shoots, early morning (7–9 AM) golden hour and late afternoon (4–6 PM) provide the most flattering, magazine-quality natural light with minimal harsh shadows.

08

Build a Digital + Print Portfolio

In today's market, models need both: a digital portfolio (Instagram/website/PDF) for agencies, and printed comp cards for in-person casting calls. We deliver files optimised for both formats.

09

Understand What Agencies Look For

Modelling agencies in Delhi and Mumbai look for versatility, professionalism, and clean images that show your natural features. Avoid over-editing. Natural retouching always wins over heavy filters.

10

Invest in Regular Portfolio Updates

Top models update their portfolio every 6–12 months. As you grow in confidence, style, and body, your images should reflect that evolution. Regular shoots help you track and showcase your growth.

11

Social Media & Personal Branding

In 2024, your Instagram IS your portfolio. Post consistently, maintain a cohesive aesthetic, engage with brands, and use SEO-friendly captions. We create social-media-ready content during your shoot.

12

Research and Trust Your Photographer

Always check portfolios, reviews, and credentials before booking. Never pay large advances upfront. At Digital Story Studio, we offer a consultation call before every shoot to ensure you're completely comfortable.
