Our Creative Shoot Process — From Idea to Masterpiece

01

Creative Discovery Call

A 30–45 minute deep-dive conversation where we understand your vision, goals, aesthetic preferences, target audience, and emotional intent. We explore references, discuss themes, and identify the core story we want to tell.

02

Concept Development & Mood Board

We develop a comprehensive creative brief including mood boards, colour palettes, lighting references, wardrobe direction, prop lists, location options, and a shot list. You review and approve before a single frame is shot.

03

Pre-Production Planning

Location permits, talent coordination, prop sourcing, makeup artist briefing, equipment preparation, and call sheet creation. We handle every production detail so you arrive relaxed and ready to create.

04

Production Day — Shoot

Full creative team on set. We execute the concept methodically, while also leaving room for organic creative moments. Our director guides expression and performance while the DOP crafts each frame precisely to the mood board.

05

Post-Production & Colour Science

Offline editing, colour grading, sound design, VFX, and motion graphics. For photography: RAW processing, advanced retouching, and cohesive colour grading across the full series for editorial consistency.

06

Review, Revision & Final Delivery

Client review with up to 2 rounds of revisions included. Final delivery via Google Drive in multiple formats — web-optimised, print-ready, and platform-specific exports for Instagram, YouTube, and beyond.

Frequently Asked Questions — Creative Shoots Delhi NCR

What exactly is a creative concept shoot?

A creative concept shoot is a fully planned photography or videography session built around a specific theme, narrative, or artistic vision. Unlike standard shoots, concept shoots involve mood board development, location transformation, props, costumes, lighting design, and directorial input to create a cohesive visual story — not just isolated good-looking photos.

How much does a creative photoshoot cost in Delhi?

Creative concept photography packages start from ₹8,999 for a 3-hour themed shoot with 30 edited images. Cinematic story shoots combining photo + video start at ₹18,999. Full production projects (music videos, brand films, documentaries) are custom-quoted based on scope, crew size, and deliverables. Contact us for a tailored estimate.

I have a creative idea but don't know how to execute it — can you help?

Yes — this is exactly what we love! Many of our best projects begin with a client having an emotion or vague idea and our creative team translating that into a full visual concept. Share your inspiration, references, or even just how you want people to feel when they see the content — we'll develop the rest.

Do you do creative pre-wedding shoots in Delhi NCR?

Absolutely! Our creative pre-wedding shoots in Delhi NCR are among our most popular offerings. We craft unique, story-driven concepts for couples — from Mughal-era heritage romance to modern urban cinematic films to fantasy forest shoots. We cover Delhi, Noida, Gurgaon, Agra, Jaipur, and outstation locations.

Can you produce music videos in Delhi NCR?

Yes — music video production is one of our specializations. We have produced music videos for independent artists, emerging bands, and established artists across Delhi NCR. We handle everything from concept, script, storyboard, location, art direction, shoot, edit, colour grade, VFX, and audio sync to YouTube-optimised final delivery.

How long does it take to deliver creative shoot content?

Standard creative concept photography: 7 working days. Cinematic story shoots (photo + video): 10–14 working days. Full production projects: 3–6 weeks depending on scope. Rush delivery available. All timelines are agreed in writing before the shoot.

What are the best creative shoot locations in Delhi?

Delhi offers incredible diversity for creative shoots. Our favourites include: Lodhi Garden & Humayun's Tomb (heritage romance), Hauz Khas Village (bohemian urban), Agrasen ki Baoli (surreal/dark aesthetics), Champa Gali (cosy, café aesthetic), Yamuna Ghat at golden hour (spiritual & dramatic), and industrial North Delhi (gritty, editorial). We also scout custom locations based on your concept.
