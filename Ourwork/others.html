Frequently Asked Questions

What photography and videography services does Digital Story Studio offer in Delhi NCR?

We offer a complete range of visual services: Wedding Photography & Videography, Wedding Highlights & Teaser Films, Same Day Wedding Edits (SDE), Live Streaming for weddings & events, Events Photography & Videography, Model Photoshoot & Portfolio Photography, Creative Concept Shoots, and Destination Wedding Videography. All services are available across Delhi, Noida, Gurgaon, Faridabad, and Ghaziabad.

How far in advance should I book Digital Story Studio for my wedding?

We recommend booking 3–6 months in advance for weddings during peak season (October–February) as our calendar fills up quickly. For events and non-wedding shoots, bookings 2–4 weeks in advance are typically sufficient. However, we accommodate last-minute bookings based on availability — contact us directly for urgent requirements.

Do you travel outside Delhi NCR for shoots?

Yes! We regularly travel for destination weddings, outstation events, and location-specific shoots across India — Goa, Jaipur, Udaipur, Mumbai, Kerala, Shimla, Mussoorie, and internationally to Dubai, Bali, Thailand, Singapore, and more. Travel and accommodation costs are added to the package and quoted transparently upfront.

What is the pricing for your photography and videography packages in Delhi?

Our pricing varies by service: Model shoots from ₹5,999, Event photography from ₹8,999, Creative shoots from ₹8,999, Wedding packages from ₹50,000. We offer customised packages based on your specific requirements, duration, and scope. Contact us via WhatsApp or call for a personalised quote tailored to your event.

How long does it take to receive the final photos and videos?

Delivery times by service: Wedding Teasers: 72 hours, SDE: same night, Event photography: 24–48 hours, Wedding highlights: 2–4 weeks, Complete wedding film: 60–90 days, Model shoots: 5–7 days, Creative shoots: 7–14 days. Rush delivery is available on most services at a nominal additional charge.
