Our team arrives 3 hours before the event to set up all cameras, audio equipment, video switcher, encoding systems, and streaming hardware. We conduct a full test broadcast to verify quality, check platform connectivity, and share the stream link with your guests.

Frequently Asked Questions — Live Streaming Services Delhi NCR

How reliable is your live streaming connection at indoor venues in Delhi NCR?

We guarantee 99% uptime using professional cellular bonding routers that combine multiple 4G/5G SIM cards and venue WiFi into a single high-bandwidth, highly redundant connection. Even if one connection fails, others instantly take over without any interruption in the stream. We have successfully streamed from venues with very poor native internet connectivity across Delhi, Noida, Gurgaon, and Faridabad using this technology.

Can unlimited guests watch the live stream simultaneously?

Yes! When streaming to YouTube Live, the platform supports unlimited concurrent viewers. For Facebook Live, Instagram, and Zoom, the simultaneous viewer capacity is also very large. We have hosted streams with up to 50,000 concurrent viewers for large events. Private platform streams are set up based on expected audience size to ensure smooth playback for all viewers globally.

Is the live stream recording available to keep after the wedding?

Absolutely. All live streams are simultaneously recorded locally in 4K on our professional storage devices, independent of the internet connection. You receive the complete recorded video as a digital file after the event — typically within 24–48 hours via a private cloud link. The recording is permanent and can be downloaded, shared, and archived by your family forever.

Can you stream a wedding that includes multiple events — Mehendi, Sangeet, and main wedding?

Yes! We offer multi-day, multi-event streaming packages for complete wedding coverage including Mehendi, Sangeet, Haldi, Cocktail, Wedding, and Reception. Each event gets its own stream/recording. Family members who missed one event can still watch that day's recording later. Our multi-event packages are competitively priced and widely popular among NRI couples planning weddings in Delhi NCR.

Do you provide live streaming for corporate events in Delhi NCR?

Yes! Our team provides professional corporate event live streaming in Delhi NCR — covering product launches, annual general meetings, town halls, conferences, seminars, award ceremonies, and company events. We can integrate corporate branding (lower thirds, name tags, logo overlays) into the live stream, provide interactive Q&A capabilities via YouTube/Zoom, and deliver the recording in corporate-ready formats for internal and external distribution.

How much does wedding live streaming cost in Delhi NCR?

Live streaming pricing depends on the duration of streaming, number of cameras deployed, platform requirements, venue location in NCR, and whether multiple events are being streamed. We offer competitive packages starting at affordable rates for basic single-camera YouTube streams to comprehensive multi-camera, multi-platform broadcast packages for grand weddings.
