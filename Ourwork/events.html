Frequently Asked Questions — Events Coverage Delhi NCR

How early should I book event photography/videography in Delhi NCR?

We recommend booking your event coverage at least 2–4 weeks in advance for corporate events and social functions. For large-scale events requiring multiple camera operators and lighting setups, booking 4–8 weeks in advance is ideal. For urgent same-day or last-minute event bookings in Delhi NCR, contact us via WhatsApp — we often accommodate urgent requests subject to team availability.

What is the difference between event photography and event videography?

Event photography produces high-resolution still images — candid moments, group photos, stage coverage, product displays — ideal for corporate reports, social media, and print media. Event videography creates video coverage including highlight reels, interviews, stage performances, and full event recordings — ideal for YouTube, internal communications, training materials, and broadcast. We provide both services simultaneously with separate dedicated teams to ensure neither is compromised.

Do you provide coverage for outdoor events in Delhi NCR?

Yes! We regularly cover outdoor events across Delhi NCR — garden parties, terrace events, sports events, farmhouse celebrations, open-air concerts, and outdoor exhibitions. Our team brings portable professional lighting for evening events, weather-sealed cameras for unpredictable outdoor conditions, and wireless audio equipment for outdoor audio capture. We have covered events in open lawns in Gurgaon, Surajkund grounds in Faridabad, Okhla riverside in Delhi, and outdoor venues across all NCR districts.

Can you provide same-day event content delivery for social media?

Absolutely! Our rapid delivery service for social media events includes: edited highlight photos delivered within 2–4 hours, Instagram Stories/Reels-ready content within 3–4 hours, and a 60–90 second event recap video within 6–8 hours. This service is especially popular for product launches, brand events, fashion shows, and corporate functions in Delhi, Noida, and Gurgaon where social media amplification is a key event objective.

Do you provide corporate event photography and videography packages?

Yes! We offer comprehensive corporate event packages tailored for company requirements, including: annual day coverage, conference photography and videography, employee recognition event coverage, product launch production, CSR event documentation, and executive portrait sessions.
