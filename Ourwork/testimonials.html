Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about booking Digital Story Studio

How do I know the reviews are genuine?

All our reviews are collected on verified third-party platforms — Google My Business, Facebook, JustDial and Instagram. Google and Facebook reviews are linked to real user accounts and cannot be faked or purchased. You can verify all our Google reviews directly at our Google Business Profile. We also encourage all clients to share honest feedback after their event.

Can I speak with a past client before booking?

Absolutely! We're happy to share references from recent clients who are willing to speak about their experience. WhatsApp us at +91 8810696407 and we'll connect you with a past client from a similar event type or location. We're confident they'll have nothing but great things to say!

What happens if I'm not happy with the photos or video?

Client satisfaction is our top priority. If you're not completely happy with any aspect of the delivery, we offer free revisions until you're satisfied. In the rare case of a genuine quality issue, we work with you to find the best resolution — including re-edits, partial refunds or re-shoots where possible. This commitment is reflected in our near-perfect review score.

How quickly will I receive my photos and videos after the event?

Teaser/highlights: 24–72 hours. Full edited video: 7–15 working days. Same Day Edit: delivered same evening at the reception. Photo gallery: 5–7 working days. These timelines are guaranteed and mentioned in your booking confirmation. Rush delivery options are also available at a small additional charge.

Do you cover locations outside Delhi NCR?

Yes! While our primary service area is Delhi NCR, we cover destination weddings and events across India — Rajasthan, Goa, Kerala, Himachal Pradesh, Uttarakhand and more. International destination shoots can also be arranged. Travel and accommodation charges apply for outstation events. Contact us for a customised quote for your specific location.

How do I leave a review after my shoot?

We'd love to hear from you! After your event, we send a follow-up WhatsApp message with a direct link to leave a Google Review. You can also tag us on Instagram @digitalstorystudio or post on our Facebook page. Your honest feedback helps future clients make confident decisions and inspires our team to keep delivering exceptional work.
