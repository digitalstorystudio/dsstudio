How Same Day Edit Works — Hour by Hour

A seamlessly orchestrated workflow that runs in parallel with your wedding celebrations

6:00 AM

Camera Team Arrives & Bridal Preparations Begin

Our camera team arrives 2 hours before events start. We set up editing workstation, establish footage transfer systems (wireless, high-speed), and begin filming bridal preparations — makeup, getting ready, family moments. Footage transfers wirelessly to the editing setup in real time.

8:00 AM

Editor Begins Rough Assembly Simultaneously

While ceremonies unfold, our senior SDE editor begins assembling the rough cut — selecting the most powerful moments, syncing audio, and arranging the narrative arc. Music tracks are laid down in real time as footage arrives from the live set.

12:00 PM

Pheras, Jaimala & Key Ceremony Moments Captured

Three camera operators cover the main ceremony simultaneously — one with gimbal for smooth wide shots, one for close-up reactions, one for candid moments. Every vow, every exchange of garlands, every glance is captured and immediately transferred to the editor.

2:00 PM

Color Grading & Final Polish During Lunch Break

As guests enjoy lunch, the editor applies DaVinci Resolve color grading, fine-tunes the edit, adds subtle motion graphics/lower thirds, and finalizes audio mixing. This is the most intensive 2-hour window of the entire SDE process.

5:00 PM

Final Review & Export

The completed SDE film is reviewed by the lead videographer, any quick refinements are made, and the final export is rendered in 4K. The file is transferred to the AV team at your venue for projection — or directly connected to the big screen setup.

8:00 PM

🎬 THE SCREENING — Reception Magic Moment

The lights dim. The music swells. Your wedding film from this very morning plays on the big screen to 300 of your closest family and friends. The room erupts. This is the moment. This is why SDE exists. Filmed reactions are captured for your social media content later.

Frequently Asked Questions — Same Day Edit (SDE) in Delhi NCR

What exactly is Same Day Edit (SDE) in wedding videography?

Same Day Edit (SDE) is a premium wedding videography service where a dedicated editor works on-site during your wedding, editing footage in real time so that a polished 3–5 minute highlight film is ready to be screened at your reception on the very same day as your ceremony. It's like watching a movie about your wedding while still at your wedding — creating an unforgettable emotional experience for you, your partner, and all your guests. Digital Story Studio offers the best SDE service in Delhi, Noida, Gurgaon, Faridabad, and all NCR locations.

How long does a Same Day Edit film typically run?

An SDE film is typically 3 to 5 minutes long — tightly edited, emotionally curated, and perfectly synced to music. This is the ideal length for a reception screening: long enough to cover all key moments (preparations, baraat, jaimala, pheras, candid emotions, family moments), and short enough to maintain peak emotional engagement from all guests. The SDE is then followed by the full wedding film delivered within 4–6 weeks.

Does the SDE replace the full wedding film?

No — the SDE is an additional service, not a replacement. You still receive your complete full-length wedding film (90–180 minutes), polished highlight reel (5–8 min), and social media teasers as part of your standard package. The SDE is a bonus cinematic experience created and screened in real time on your wedding day. Both the SDE and standard deliverables are included when you book Digital Story Studio's Platinum SDE package.

What technical setup is required at my venue for SDE screening?

We require a projection screen or large LED display at the reception venue (minimum 10 feet wide for best impact), an HDMI input, adequate audio system, and a small private workspace (a back room, corridor, or manager's office) for our editing setup. Most premium venues in Delhi, Noida, and Gurgaon already have all this equipment. We coordinate with your venue AV team in advance to ensure seamless technical setup. No special arrangements are typically needed.

Can SDE be done for multi-day weddings (Mehendi, Sangeet, Wedding)?

Absolutely! We offer SDE for any single event within a multi-day wedding — most commonly for the main wedding day (ceremony + reception). We can also create individual SDE films for Sangeet nights and other major events if time permits. For multi-day SDEs, we bring additional editing staff to maintain quality. This service is available at all Delhi NCR farmhouses, banquet halls, hotels, and outdoor venues.

How much does Same Day Edit wedding videography cost in Delhi?

SDE is a premium add-on service priced based on the wedding's complexity, venue location within NCR, team size required, and technical setup needs. We offer custom quotes for every wedding. The investment is worth every rupee — the SDE screening consistently becomes the most talked-about moment of any wedding. Contact us via WhatsApp or the form below for a detailed custom quote for your specific Delhi NCR wedding venue and date.

Is the SDE file available to keep and share after the wedding?

Yes! After the reception screening, we make final quality checks and deliver the SDE film digitally within 24–48 hours via a private cloud link. You receive it in 4K quality (for archiving), 1080p (for streaming), and an Instagram-optimized vertical version. Many couples share their SDE on social media within 48 hours and it consistently generates thousands of views, reactions, and comments from friends and family across India.
