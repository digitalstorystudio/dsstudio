About Digital Story Studio

Our Story

Digital Story Studio was founded by Amish Raaz, a Delhi-based cinematographer with over 8 years of professional wedding filmmaking experience. What began as a solo passion project has grown into one of Delhi NCR's most trusted wedding film studios — built entirely on referrals, reviews, and real results.

Over the years, Amish and the Digital Story Studio team have filmed 500+ weddings across India — from intimate home ceremonies in Laxminagar to grand destination weddings in Jaipur, Goa, Udaipur, and Dubai. Every project, regardless of size, gets the same cinematic attention to detail.

The studio's signature style is cinematic, emotional, and story-driven — less about posed moments, more about real ones. We look for the grandmother wiping a tear, the bride's nervous laugh, the groom's steady exhale before he sees her. Those are the frames that matter twenty years later.

Our Mission

Our mission is straightforward: deliver cinematic wedding films that couples actually rewatch. Not a 45-minute documentary nobody sits through, but a tight, emotionally honest film that puts you right back inside your wedding day every time you press play.

We stay lean on purpose — Amish leads every shoot personally, not a junior cameraperson. When you book Digital Story Studio, you get the person whose work you saw on the portfolio, not a replacement. That commitment to consistency is why over 90% of our bookings come through word-of-mouth.

Gear & Technology

We invest in professional-grade equipment so your wedding film holds up on any screen — from your phone to a 4K TV at a family screening. Our current production kit includes:

  • Sony FX3 — full-frame cinema camera for rich, low-light cinematic footage
  • Sony A7S III — backup body, unmatched high-ISO performance for dark mandaps and reception halls
  • DJI Mavic 3 Pro — drone for sweeping aerial coverage of venues and baraat processions
  • DJI RS 3 Pro Gimbal — smooth handheld movement for candid moments
  • Wireless audio systems — vows and pandit's words captured clearly, even in open-air mandaps
  • 4K/8K delivery — final films delivered in cinema-grade quality, future-proofed for years
